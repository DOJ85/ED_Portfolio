One of the feature in DayZ Expansion is the rework I have done to some areas of the map.
Inspired by The Last of Us environment design, I have transformed some locations to make them look overgrown and add a bit of lore behind some of them.
I have spent countless hours working on it, and restaring when I wasn't happy with the result, and learned most of what I know about mapping on this project.

KarmaKrew's Servers:
Dolnik transformed into a military base.

Requirements: Tranform Dolnik into an abandoned mining town that was used by the military. Need to be very dynamic for pvp, and make use of the industrial pipes.

After Zero Server:
VMC Military Depot.

Requirements: Add obstacles to VMC to add spice to PvP in that area. It also needs to be surrounded by a forest to make it harder to snipe and more cover in general. Made it look like it was used as a depot/junkyard for military vehicles and gear.
